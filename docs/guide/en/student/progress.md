# Progress & Achievements

Track your Quran memorization milestones, earn badges, compete on leaderboards, and monitor your learning journey.

## Progress Overview

Navigate to **Progress** to view your comprehensive learning analytics.

### Key Metrics Dashboard

**Memorization Statistics:**
- **Total Memorized** — Percentage of Quran memorized (e.g., 35.5%)
- **Pages Memorized** — Number out of 604 total pages
- **Verses Memorized** — Number out of 6,236 total verses
- **Juz Completed** — Full Juz mastered (e.g., 10/30)
- **Surahs Completed** — Full surahs memorized (e.g., 15/114)

**Consistency Metrics:**
- **Current Streak** — Consecutive days/sessions with activity
- **Longest Streak** — Your personal best streak
- **Total Sessions Attended** — Lifetime attendance count
- **Average Weekly Progress** — Pages/verses per week

**Performance Metrics:**
- **Average Session Grade** — Mean score across all sessions
- **Average Exam Score** — Mean score across all exams
- **Average Tajweed Score** — Mean Tajweed evaluation
- **Improvement Trend** — Improving, stable, or declining indicator

## Custom Goals

### Setting Personal Goals

Create custom goals beyond the standard milestones:

**Goal Types:**
- **Memorization Goals** — "Memorize Surah Al-Kahf by Ramadan"
- **Performance Goals** — "Achieve 90+ average this term"
- **Attendance Goals** — "Perfect attendance this month"
- **Tajweed Goals** — "Improve Tajweed score to 95+"
- **Review Goals** — "Review entire memorized portion monthly"

### Creating a Goal

To set a custom goal:

1. Navigate to **Progress** > **Goals**
2. Click **Create New Goal**
3. Fill in goal details:
   - **Title** — Goal name
   - **Description** — What you want to achieve
   - **Target** — Specific metric and value
   - **Deadline** — Target completion date
   - **Category** — Memorization, performance, attendance, etc.
4. Click **Save Goal**

### Tracking Goal Progress

**Goal Card Shows:**
- Goal title and description
- Progress bar (% complete)
- Current status vs. target
- Days remaining
- Projected completion date (based on current pace)

**Progress Updates:**
- Automatic updates based on your activity
- Manual check-ins (optional)
- Milestone markers
- Completion celebration when achieved

### Goal Completion

When you achieve a goal:
- Celebration notification
- Goal moved to "Completed" section
- Achievement recorded in timeline
- Option to set new, more challenging goal
