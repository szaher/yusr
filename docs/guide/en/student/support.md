# Support & Communication

Get help, submit tickets, request leave, manage notifications, and stay updated with academy announcements.

## Support Tickets

### Creating a Support Ticket

Need help? Submit a support ticket:

1. Navigate to **Support** or **Tickets**
2. Click **Create New Ticket**
3. Fill in ticket details:
   - **Category** — Select issue type:
     - Technical Issue
     - Account Problem
     - Billing/Enrollment
     - Academic Question
     - General Inquiry
   - **Subject** — Brief description of issue
   - **Description** — Detailed explanation of your problem or question
   - **Priority** — Normal, High, Urgent
   - **Attachments** — Screenshots, documents, or files (optional)
4. Click **Submit Ticket**

**What to Include:**
- Clear description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (for technical issues)
- What you've already tried
- Screenshots if relevant
- Expected vs. actual behavior

### Support Categories

**Technical Issues:**
- Login problems
- App not loading
- Features not working
- Browser compatibility
- Audio/video issues in sessions
- Mobile app problems

**Account Problems:**
- Profile information updates
- Password reset issues
- Email change requests
- Notification settings
- Privacy concerns

**Academic Questions:**
- Memorization plan questions
- Assignment clarification
- Grade inquiries
- Schedule questions
- Group placement

**General Inquiries:**
- Academy policies
- Feature requests
- Feedback and suggestions
- General questions

### Ticket Priority Levels

| Priority | Response Time | Use When |
|----------|--------------|----------|
| **Normal** | 1-3 business days | Non-urgent questions, general inquiries |
| **High** | 12-24 hours | Affects your ability to complete work |
| **Urgent** | 2-4 hours | Critical issues preventing access or participation |

> **Note:** Urgent priority should be reserved for genuine emergencies. Misuse may affect future ticket processing.

## Viewing Ticket Status

### Ticket Dashboard

View all your tickets:

**Open Tickets**
- Active tickets awaiting response
- Your pending replies
- Under investigation

**Closed Tickets**
- Resolved issues
- Ticket history
- Solutions provided

### Ticket Details

Click any ticket to view:

| Information | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Ticket ID** | Unique reference number |
| **Status** | Open, In Progress, Waiting for Reply, Resolved, Closed |
| **Created** | Date and time submitted |
| **Category** | Issue type |
| **Priority** | Normal, High, Urgent |
| **Assigned To** | Support staff member handling your ticket |
| **Last Updated** | Most recent activity |

### Ticket Status Meanings

**Open**
- Ticket submitted, awaiting support review
- No action taken yet

**In Progress**
- Support staff investigating
- Solution being prepared

**Waiting for Reply**
- Support requested more information from you
- Check and respond to keep ticket active

**Resolved**
- Support provided solution
- Confirm if issue is fixed or reopen if needed

**Closed**
- Issue fully resolved
- Ticket archived
- Can reference for future issues

## Ticket Responses and Updates

### Replying to Tickets

To respond to support staff:

1. Open the ticket
2. Read support's message
3. Scroll to **Reply** section
4. Type your response
5. Attach files if needed
6. Click **Send Reply**

**Response Notifications:**
- Email notification when support replies
- Push notification (if enabled)
- Unread badge on tickets icon

### Closing a Ticket

When your issue is resolved:

1. Open the ticket
2. Confirm the solution worked
3. Click **Mark as Resolved** or **Close Ticket**
4. Optional: Rate the support experience
5. Optional: Leave feedback

### Reopening a Ticket

If issue persists after resolution:

1. Open the closed ticket
2. Click **Reopen Ticket**
3. Explain why the issue isn't resolved
4. Submit
5. Ticket returns to "Open" status

## Leave Requests

### Requesting Leave

Need to miss a session? Submit a leave request:

1. Navigate to **Support** or **Leave Requests**
2. Click **Request Leave**
3. Fill in request form:
   - **Session(s)** — Select session(s) you'll miss
   - **Start Date** — First day of absence
   - **End Date** — Last day of absence (if multiple)
   - **Reason** — Explain why you need leave:
     - Personal/family matter
     - Medical reason
     - Travel
     - Emergency
     - Other (specify)
   - **Supporting Documents** — Medical note, travel docs (if applicable)
4. Click **Submit Request**

### Leave Request Types

**Single Session Leave**
- Missing one session
- Typically auto-approved if submitted 24+ hours in advance

**Extended Leave**
- Missing multiple sessions
- Requires moderator approval
- May require make-up plan

**Emergency Leave**
- Submitted less than 24 hours before session
- Explain emergency circumstances
- Subject to moderator discretion

### Leave Request Approval Process

**Approval Timeline:**
- ≥24 hours advance: Usually approved within 2-4 hours
- <24 hours: Requires moderator review ASAP
- Emergency: Contact moderator directly

**Approval Criteria:**
- Valid reason provided
- Advance notice (when possible)
- Not excessive leave history
- Supporting documentation (if required)

### Viewing Leave Request Status

**Status Indicators:**

| Status | Meaning | Next Steps |
|--------|---------|------------|
| **Pending** | Awaiting moderator review | Wait for response |
| **Approved** | Leave granted | Absence will be marked as "Excused" |
| **Denied** | Leave not approved | Reason provided, attendance required |
| **Cancelled** | You cancelled the request | Normal attendance expected |

### Leave Request History

View all leave requests:
- Dates and sessions
- Approval status
- Reasons provided
- Approver comments
- Total leave days taken

**Leave Statistics:**
- Total leave days this term
- Approved vs. denied ratio
- Most common leave reasons
- Leave balance (if applicable)

## Notifications

### Notification Center

Access notifications:

1. Click the **bell icon** in top navigation
2. View all notifications
3. Badge shows unread count

### Notification Types

**Session Notifications:**
- Session scheduled
- Session reminder (1 hour before)
- Session starting now
- Session cancelled/rescheduled
- Grade posted

**Assignment Notifications:**
- New assignment created
- Assignment due soon (3 days, 1 day)
- Assignment overdue
- Assignment graded

**Exam Notifications:**
- Exam available
- Exam due soon
- Exam results available

**Support Notifications:**
- Ticket reply received
- Ticket status changed
- Leave request approved/denied

**Achievement Notifications:**
- Milestone reached
- Badge earned
- Leaderboard rank changed

**Announcement Notifications:**
- New academy announcement
- Important updates
- System maintenance

### Managing Notifications

**Notification Actions:**
- **Mark as Read** — Clear individual notification
- **Mark All as Read** — Clear all notifications
- **Delete** — Remove notification
- **Go to Source** — Jump to relevant page (session, assignment, etc.)

### Notification Settings

Customize which notifications you receive:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Toggle notification types:
   - Sessions
   - Assignments
   - Exams
   - Support tickets
   - Achievements
   - Announcements
3. Choose delivery methods:
   - **Email** — Sent to registered email
   - **Push** — Mobile/desktop notifications (requires PWA)
   - **In-App** — Notification center only
4. Set quiet hours (optional):
   - Don't send notifications during specified hours
   - Example: 10 PM - 7 AM
5. Click **Save Preferences**

### Notification Preferences by Type

**Recommended Settings:**

| Notification Type | Email | Push | In-App |
|------------------|-------|------|--------|
| Session Reminders | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| New Assignments | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Assignment Due | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Exam Available | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Grades Posted | ✓ | ○ | ✓ |
| Achievements | ○ | ○ | ✓ |
| Announcements | ✓ | ○ | ✓ |
| Ticket Replies | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

✓ = Recommended to enable  
○ = Optional based on preference

## Announcements

### Viewing Announcements

Access academy announcements:

1. Navigate to **Announcements** from sidebar
2. Or view announcement panel on dashboard
3. Unread announcements highlighted

### Announcement Types

**System Announcements:**
- Platform updates
- New features
- Scheduled maintenance
- Policy changes

**Academy Announcements:**
- Important dates (holidays, exam periods)
- Event information
- Enrollment updates
- General news

**Group Announcements:**
- From your moderator
- Group-specific information
- Schedule changes
- Reminders

### Announcement Details

Each announcement shows:
- **Title** — Brief headline
- **Category** — System, Academy, or Group
- **Posted By** — Who published it
- **Date** — When posted
- **Priority** — Normal or Important
- **Content** — Full message
- **Attachments** — Documents or links (if any)

**Important Announcements:**
- Marked with ⚠️ icon
- Appear at top of list
- May require acknowledgment
- Dismissal tracked

### Announcement Actions

**Actions You Can Take:**
- **Mark as Read** — Acknowledge you've seen it
- **Pin** — Keep at top of your list
- **Archive** — Move to archived announcements
- **Share** — Send link to parent/guardian
- **Download Attachments** — Save attached documents

### Announcement Notifications

**When Announcements Are Posted:**
- In-app notification appears
- Email sent (if enabled in settings)
- Push notification (if enabled)
- Badge on announcements icon

**Muting Announcements:**
- Can't disable system/important announcements
- Can reduce frequency of general announcements in settings

## Messaging (if available)

### Direct Messages

Some academies enable student-moderator messaging:

**Sending a Message:**
1. Navigate to **Messages**
2. Click **New Message**
3. Select recipient (your moderator)
4. Type message
5. Attach files if needed
6. Click **Send**

**Message Etiquette:**
- Be respectful and professional
- Clear subject line
- Concise message
- Response time: 24-48 hours
- Use tickets for urgent technical issues
