# Sessions

View, join, and track your attendance for live memorization sessions with your halaqah group.

## Session Overview

Sessions are the core of your Yusr Academy experience — live virtual classes where you recite to your moderator, receive feedback, and learn with your peers.

### Session Types

**Regular Sessions**
- Weekly or bi-weekly classes
- Recitation and review
- New memorization assessment
- Group learning activities

**Review Sessions**
- Special revision classes
- Consolidate previous memorization
- Address common mistakes
- Tajweed focus

**Exam Sessions**
- Formal assessment periods
- Graded evaluations
- Progress checkpoints

## Viewing Sessions

### Sessions List

Navigate to **Sessions** to view:

**Upcoming Sessions**

**Past Sessions**

## Joining a Session

### Before the Session

**Preparation Checklist:**
1. ✓ Complete homework assignment
2. ✓ Review previous memorization
3. ✓ Test your internet connection
4. ✓ Have your Mushaf ready
5. ✓ Find a quiet location
6. ✓ Ensure microphone/camera work

**Notification Reminders:**
- 24 hours before: Email reminder
- 1 hour before: Push notification (if enabled)
- 15 minutes before: Final reminder

### Joining the Virtual Classroom

To join a session:

1. Navigate to **Sessions**
2. Find your upcoming session
3. Click **Join Session** or click the meeting link
4. You'll be redirected to the video conferencing platform
5. Allow microphone and camera permissions
6. Join the waiting room (moderator will admit you)

**Meeting Link Availability:**
- Links become active 15 minutes before session start
- Links remain accessible for 30 minutes after scheduled end time
- Late arrivals can join in progress

> **Technical Tip:** Join 5 minutes early to resolve any technical issues before class starts.

### During the Session

**Student Responsibilities:**
1. Arrive on time
2. Mute microphone when not reciting
3. Keep camera on (unless technical issues)
4. Wait for your turn to recite
5. Follow moderator instructions
6. Participate in group activities

**Recitation Process:**
1. Moderator calls your name
2. Unmute your microphone
3. State the range you're reciting (e.g., "Surah Al-Baqarah, verses 1-10")
4. Recite clearly and at a moderate pace
5. Wait for feedback and correction
6. Note any mistakes or Tajweed points
7. Mute microphone when finished

## Session Attendance

### Attendance Status

Your attendance is marked as one of four statuses:

**Present** ✓
- You attended the session on time
- Contributes to attendance streak
- Eligible for session grade

**Late** ⏰
- You joined after session started
- Still counts as attended
- May affect participation grade
- Counts toward attendance streak

**Absent** ✗
- You did not attend the session
- Breaks attendance streak
- No grade assigned
- Requires make-up work

**Excused** 📋
- Pre-approved absence via leave request
- Does not break attendance streak
- May require make-up work
- Documented reason on file

### Viewing Attendance Record

To view your attendance history:

1. Navigate to **Sessions** or **Attendance**
2. View attendance table with all sessions
3. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Status (present, absent, late, excused)
   - Session type
4. Export attendance report (if available)

**Attendance Statistics:**
- **Total Sessions** — Sessions you were scheduled for
- **Attended** — Present + Late
- **Absent** — Unexcused absences
- **Excused** — Approved leave
- **Attendance Rate** — (Attended / Total) × 100

### Improving Attendance

**Best Practices:**
- Set calendar reminders for sessions
- Enable push notifications
- Communicate with moderator if unable to attend
- Submit leave requests in advance
- Maintain a consistent schedule

> **Important:** Chronic absence may result in removal from your group. Aim for 90%+ attendance.

## Session Grades

### Receiving Grades

After each session, your moderator assigns grades based on:

**Recitation Performance (60%)**
- Accuracy of memorization
- Fluency and confidence
- Correction of previous mistakes

**Tajweed Application (30%)**
- Correct pronunciation (Makharij)
- Application of Tajweed rules
- Quality of recitation

**Participation (10%)**
- Engagement in class
- Preparation and readiness
- Respect and conduct

### Grade Scale

Typical grading scale:

| Score | Grade | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 90-100 | A+ | Excellent, exceptional performance |
| 80-89 | A | Very good, strong understanding |
| 70-79 | B | Good, satisfactory performance |
| 60-69 | C | Acceptable, needs improvement |
| Below 60 | F | Unsatisfactory, significant issues |

> **Note:** Grading scales may vary by moderator or program. Check with your teacher for specific criteria.

## Requesting Leave

### Advance Notice

If you cannot attend a session:

1. Navigate to **Support** or **Leave Requests**
2. Click **Request Leave**
3. Select the session(s) you'll miss
4. Provide a reason for absence
5. Submit request

**Approval Process:**
- Requests submitted ≥24 hours in advance: Usually auto-approved
- Requests submitted <24 hours: Require moderator approval
- Emergency absences: Contact moderator directly

See the [Support Guide](./support.md) for detailed leave request instructions.

### Make-Up Work

For excused absences:

1. Your moderator may assign make-up work
2. Complete assignments by the specified deadline
3. Schedule a make-up recitation (if required)
4. Grades from make-up work will be recorded

## Session Schedule

### Viewing Your Schedule

To see all upcoming sessions:

1. Navigate to **Sessions**
2. View in list or calendar format
3. Add to personal calendar:
   - Download .ics file
   - Copy meeting link
   - Set personal reminders

### Schedule Changes

If a session is rescheduled or cancelled:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. In-app notification will appear
3. Updated details will show in sessions list
4. Meeting link may change

**Reasons for Changes:**
- Moderator unavailability
- Holiday observances
- System maintenance
- Emergency cancellations

> **Stay Informed:** Check your sessions page regularly and enable notifications to stay updated on schedule changes.

## Timezone Handling

All session times are displayed in your local timezone based on your account settings.

**To update your timezone:**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Profile**
2. Select **Timezone**
3. Choose your timezone from the list
4. Save changes
5. All session times will update automatically

> **Travel Tip:** Update your timezone when traveling to ensure correct session times.
