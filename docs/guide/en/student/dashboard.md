# Student Dashboard

Your dashboard is your home base in Yusr Academy, providing an at-a-glance view of your Quran memorization journey.

## Dashboard Overview

The student dashboard displays key information organized into widgets:

### Group Information

**Your Halaqah Details**
- **Level** — Your current program level
- **Class** — Your assigned class within the level
- **Group** — Your specific memorization circle (حلقة)
- **Moderator** — Your teacher's name and contact

This widget shows which group you belong to and who your moderator is. Click on your moderator's name to send them a message.

### Memorization Progress

**Your Progress at a Glance**
- **Progress Percentage** — Overall Quran memorization completion (e.g., 23.5%)
- **Visual Progress Bar** — Color-coded progress indicator
- **Current Position** — Your current location in the Quran (e.g., Juz 12, Hizb 3)

This widget updates automatically as your moderator logs your memorization reviews.

### Attendance Streak

**Your Consistency Record**
- **Current Streak** — Number of consecutive sessions attended
- **Best Streak** — Your personal record for consecutive attendance
- **Total Sessions** — Total number of sessions you've attended
- **Attendance Rate** — Percentage of sessions attended

> **Tip:** Maintaining a strong attendance streak can unlock special badges and achievements!

### Average Exam Score

**Academic Performance**
- **Overall Average** — Your average score across all exams
- **Recent Trend** — Indicator showing if your scores are improving, stable, or declining
- **Total Exams** — Number of exams completed

### Recent Grade History

**Performance Over Time**
- **Chart View** — Visual graph showing your last 10 session grades
- **Grade Range** — Typical grading scale from 0-100
- **Trend Line** — Shows if your performance is improving

Use this chart to identify patterns in your performance and areas for improvement.

### Upcoming Sessions

**What's Next**
- **Next Session Date & Time** — Converted to your local timezone
- **Session Type** — Regular class, review session, or exam
- **Meeting Link** — Click to join the virtual classroom
- **Countdown Timer** — Time remaining until the session

> **Note:** Session reminders are sent via email and push notification (if enabled) 1 hour before start time.

### Assignment Completion

**Your Current Workload**
- **Pending Assignments** — Number of incomplete assignments
- **Completed Assignments** — Assignments you've finished
- **Completion Rate** — Percentage of on-time completions
- **Upcoming Deadlines** — Next 3 assignments due

Click **View All Assignments** to see details and mark assignments complete.

## Dashboard Actions

### Quick Actions Menu

Common tasks accessible from your dashboard:

1. **View Memorization Plan** — See your complete memorization schedule
2. **Join Next Session** — Quick link to join your upcoming virtual class
3. **Complete Assignment** — Mark an assignment as finished
4. **Take Available Exam** — Access exams your moderator has assigned
5. **Submit Support Ticket** — Get help from academy staff
6. **Request Leave** — Request absence for upcoming sessions

### Notification Center

Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation to:

- View recent notifications
- Mark notifications as read
- Filter by type (sessions, assignments, exams, announcements)
- Clear all notifications

### Dashboard Refresh

The dashboard updates automatically when:

- New grades are posted
- Assignments are created or updated
- Session times are scheduled
- Milestones are achieved
- Announcements are published

> **Performance Tip:** If data appears outdated, pull down to refresh on mobile or click the refresh icon.

## Understanding Your Stats

### Memorization Percentage

Calculated based on:
- Total pages/verses memorized
- Total pages/verses in the Quran (604 pages)
- Formula: `(Memorized Pages / 604) × 100`

### Attendance Rate

Calculated as:
- Formula: `(Sessions Attended / Total Sessions) × 100`
- Includes: Present, Late (counts as attended)
- Excludes: Absent, Excused absences

### Average Score

Calculated from:
- All graded sessions
- All completed exams
- Recent performance weighted more heavily

## Customizing Your Dashboard

### Widget Visibility

Some dashboard widgets can be hidden or rearranged:

1. Click **Customize Dashboard** (if available)
2. Toggle widgets on/off
3. Drag to reorder widgets
4. Click **Save Layout**

### Dashboard Themes

Change your dashboard appearance:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Appearance**
2. Choose from:
   - Light mode
   - Dark mode
   - Auto (follows system preference)
3. Select accent color (optional)

## Mobile Dashboard

On mobile devices, the dashboard adapts to a vertical layout:

- Widgets stack vertically
- Charts scale to screen width
- Quick actions appear in a floating button
- Swipe to refresh

## Dashboard Notifications

Red badge indicators show:

- **Notifications** — Unread notification count
- **Assignments** — Pending assignment count
- **Messages** — Unread message count
- **Announcements** — New announcement count
