# Student Progress Tracking

Monitor individual and group progress, view leaderboards, award badges, and track milestones.

## Group Progress Overview

Navigate to **Progress** to view comprehensive group analytics.

### Group Statistics Dashboard

**Memorization Progress:**
- **Average Progress** — Mean Quran percentage across all students
- **Total Juz Completed** — Cumulative Juz count
- **Total Pages Memorized** — Sum of all student pages
- **Completion Range** — Lowest to highest student progress

**Performance Metrics:**
- **Average Session Grade** — Mean grade across all sessions
- **Average Exam Score** — Mean exam performance
- **Average Tajweed Score** — Mean Tajweed evaluations
- **Grade Trends** — Improving, stable, or declining

**Engagement Metrics:**
- **Attendance Rate** — Group attendance percentage
- **Average Streak** — Mean attendance/activity streak
- **Assignment Completion** — % of assignments completed on time
- **Active Students** — Students with recent activity

**Special Badges:**
- Early Bird
- Helpful Peer
- Quran Explorer
- Ramadan Champion
- Custom badges (admin-created)

### Awarding Manual Badges

Some badges require moderator award:

1. Navigate to **Progress** > Student Profile
2. Click **Award Badge**
3. Select badge type:
   - Helpful Peer
   - Outstanding Improvement
   - Quran Explorer
   - Custom badges
4. Provide reason/justification
5. Click **Award**
6. Student receives notification

**When to Award Manual Badges:**
- Exceptional effort or improvement
- Helping other students
- Demonstrating leadership
- Special achievements not auto-tracked
- Completing special projects

### Badge Criteria Reference

**Automatic Badges** — System awards when criteria met
**Manual Badges** — Require your judgment and award

Refer to admin-configured badge criteria for specific requirements.

## Progress Reports

### Generating Student Reports

Create comprehensive progress reports:

1. Navigate to student profile
2. Click **Generate Report**
3. Select report type:
   - **Weekly Summary** — Last 7 days
   - **Monthly Report** — Full month
   - **Term Report** — Full term/semester
   - **Complete History** — All-time
4. Choose included sections:
   - Memorization progress
   - Attendance record
   - Performance analytics
   - Review history
   - Milestones and badges
5. Generate report (PDF or web view)

**Report Uses:**
- Parent communications

## Student Interventions

### Identifying Students Needing Support

**Automatic Alerts:**
- Grade below 70% average
- Attendance below 80%
- No progress in 2+ weeks
- Declining performance trend
- Streak broken (long-term)

**Manual Review:**
- Regular progress audits
- Weekly check-ins
- Monthly deep reviews

### Intervention Strategies

**Academic Support:**
- Adjust memorization pace
- Additional review sessions
- Supplemental materials
- Peer tutoring
- One-on-one time

**Attendance Issues:**
- Contact student/parent
- Identify barriers
- Flexible scheduling (if possible)
- Accountability partner

**Motivation:**
- Set short-term achievable goals
- Recognize small wins
- Personalized encouragement
- Connect to purpose

### Documenting Interventions

**Record:**
- Issue identified
- Intervention applied
- Date of intervention
- Follow-up plan
- Outcome/effectiveness

**Track Progress:**
- Monitor improvement
- Adjust approach if needed
- Escalate if necessary
- Close when resolved

### Regular Progress Monitoring

**Recognition Strategies:**
- Public acknowledgment (with permission)
