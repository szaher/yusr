# Getting Started with Yusr Academy

This guide will help you create your account, log in, and navigate the Yusr Academy platform.

## Registration

### Student Registration

To join Yusr Academy as a student:

1. Visit the Yusr Academy website
2. Click the **Register** button on the login page
3. Complete the registration form with the following information:

**Personal Information**
- **Full Name** — Your complete name in Arabic or English
- **Email Address** — A valid email you can access (used for login and notifications)
- **Password** — Choose a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
- **Date of Birth** — Your birth date
- **Gender** — Select your gender

**Contact Information**
- **Phone Number** — Your contact number (with country code)
- **Country** — Select your country of residence
- **Timezone** — Select your timezone (used for session scheduling)

**Quran Background**
- **Current Quran Level** — Select your current memorization level:
  - Beginner (just starting)
  - Some memorization (less than 5 juz)
  - Intermediate (5-15 juz)
  - Advanced (15+ juz)
  - Completed memorization
- **Tajweed Level** — Select your Tajweed proficiency:
  - Beginner
  - Intermediate
  - Advanced
- **Previous Background** — Describe any previous Quran study experience

**Additional Information**
- **Parent/Guardian Contact** — Contact information for a parent or guardian (if applicable)
- **Preferred Day** — Select your preferred day for weekly sessions

4. Review the information and click **Submit Application**
5. You will see a confirmation message that your application is under review

### Application Review Process

After submitting your registration:

1. Your application will be reviewed by academy administrators
2. You will receive an email notification once your application is reviewed
3. If approved, you will receive login credentials and group assignment
4. If additional information is needed, an administrator will contact you
5. If rejected, you will receive an explanation and guidance for reapplication

> **Tip:** Check your email spam folder if you don't receive a response within 2-3 business days.

### Moderator and Admin Accounts

Moderator and administrator accounts are created by system administrators only. If you've been assigned a moderator or admin role, you will receive login credentials via email.

## Logging In

To access your account:

1. Visit the Yusr Academy website
2. Enter your **email address** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In**
4. You will be redirected to your dashboard

### First-Time Login

On your first login:

1. Review your profile information and make any necessary updates
2. Set your notification preferences
3. Familiarize yourself with the dashboard layout
4. Complete any pending tasks or assignments

### Forgot Password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link and enter your new password
6. Confirm your new password and save

> **Security Note:** Password reset links expire after 24 hours. Request a new link if yours has expired.

## Navigating the Dashboard

### Dashboard Layout

The Yusr Academy dashboard consists of:

**Top Navigation Bar**
- Academy logo (click to return to dashboard)
- Active page title
- Notification bell icon (click to view notifications)
- User menu (profile, settings, logout)

**Sidebar Menu** (varies by role)
- Dashboard (home view)
- Role-specific menu items (sessions, assignments, exams, progress, etc.)
- Settings and support

**Main Content Area**
- Primary dashboard widgets and information
- Quick actions and recent activity
- Role-specific features

### Mobile Navigation

On mobile devices:

1. Tap the **menu icon** (three horizontal lines) to open the sidebar
2. Tap outside the sidebar to close it
3. Use the bottom navigation bar for quick access to key features

## Switching Language

Yusr Academy supports both Arabic and English:

1. Click your **user menu** in the top-right corner
2. Select **Settings** or the **language icon**
3. Choose **English** or **العربية**
4. The interface will update immediately

> **Note:** Your language preference is saved automatically and will apply to all future sessions.

## Installing as a Progressive Web App (PWA)

Install Yusr Academy on your device for offline access and a native app experience:

### On iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1. Open Yusr Academy in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with up arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Edit the name if desired, then tap **Add**
5. The Yusr Academy icon will appear on your home screen

### On Android (Chrome)

1. Open Yusr Academy in Chrome
2. Tap the **menu icon** (three dots)
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** or **Install app**
4. Confirm the installation
5. The app will be added to your home screen and app drawer

### On Desktop (Chrome, Edge)

1. Open Yusr Academy in your browser
2. Look for the **install icon** in the address bar (desktop monitor or plus sign)
3. Click the icon and select **Install**
4. The app will open in its own window

### PWA Benefits

- **Offline Access** — View cached content when offline
- **Push Notifications** — Receive updates about sessions, assignments, and announcements
- **Faster Loading** — Improved performance with cached resources
- **Native Experience** — Full-screen app without browser controls

## Notification Settings

To manage your notifications:

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Select **Notifications**
3. Toggle notifications on/off for:
   - Session reminders
   - Assignment deadlines
   - Exam availability
   - Announcements
   - Support ticket updates
   - Achievement unlocks
4. Choose notification delivery methods:
   - Email notifications
   - Push notifications (if PWA is installed)
   - In-app notifications

## Profile Settings

Update your profile information:

1. Click your **user menu**
2. Select **Profile** or **Settings**
3. Edit your information:
   - Profile photo
   - Contact details
   - Password
   - Timezone
   - Notification preferences
4. Click **Save Changes**

> **Privacy Note:** Your email address cannot be changed after registration. Contact support if you need to update your email.
